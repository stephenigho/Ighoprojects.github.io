Unilever Canteen Ordering System

This was a very unique project as it involed two canteens, manually catering to about 1500 staff, leading at least 35min on queues, food wastage, loss of productivity, staff dissatisfaction. So we recommended an automated ordering system to be developed to address the problems, which lead to a decrease in food waste by 30%. It also resulted in improved staff satisfaction.
